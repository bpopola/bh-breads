The Craft of Sourdough

At BH Breads, we believe that the best things take time. Born out of a dedication to perfect crusts and complex flavor profiles, our sourdough is crafted using traditional, slow-fermentation methods.

Every loaf is naturally leavened, shaped by hand, and baked to rustic perfection right here in Berkeley Heights, New Jersey.
