The Art of Time

At BH Breads, we believe that the best things cannot be rushed. Born out of a dedication to perfect crusts and complex flavor profiles, our sourdough is crafted using traditional, slow-fermentation methods.

Every single loaf is naturally leavened with our wild starter, shaped by hand, and baked to rustic perfection right here in Berkeley Heights, New Jersey.

Our Baking Journey

BH Breads is a small-batch startup currently in our dedicated tasting and testing phase.

While we perfect our craft, we are not currently accepting retail or wholesale orders.

We look forward to opening our doors and sharing our authentic sourdough with the community very soon!
